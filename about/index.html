Who is Justin Bean?

I read a lot. I like T-shaped things. I am a self-taught web developer with over two decades of experience and I prefer open-source technologies. I am a seasoned business strategy academic with a bachelor’s degree in marketing and professional experience in B2C, B2B and B2G industries and specialization in internet marketing/e-commerce.

With HobbyDB, I am an alumni of the 2015 class at Techstars Boulder.

I have worked with companies of all sizes throughout North America on technical and digital marketing projects in industries as diverse as private aviation, social network development, telephony, education, heavy machinery, hospitality and consumer packaged goods. I have done business with such well-known Fortune 500 companies and international organizations as Swingline, Hyatt Hotels, Mitsubishi, US Cellular, Tyson Foods, Northern Trust, Houghton Mifflin Harcourt and The Academy of Nutrition and Dietetics.

I currently live in Boulder, CO and am a co-founder and the Director of Engineering at roster.
